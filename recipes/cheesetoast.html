Cheese Toast

Description

Whether paired with a bowl of chilli, or eaten by its own, this cheesy sandwhich will please your tastebuds. This recipe will show you how to create this family favorite!

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread of your choice
  • Mayo
  • Sliced cheese of your choice
  • Canola oil

Steps

  1. Lightly oil a pan on med-low heat.
  2. Spread mayo on outer sides of two slices of bread.
  3. Add desired cheeses and top with sliced bread.
  4. Add sliced bread to pan.
  5. Flip bread after a few minutes, to ensure a golden coating.
  6. After a few minutes on the other side, remove from pan.
  7. Enjoy!
