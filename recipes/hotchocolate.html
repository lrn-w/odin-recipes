Hot Chocolate

Description

Want something warm to hold while watching your favorite holiday movie? Hot Chocolate is the best option! This recipe details how to achieve this decadent treat.

Ingredients

  • Bars of chocolate
  • Milk
  • Whipped Cream
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Chop up bar of chocolate into small pieces
  2. On low to medium heat, add chocolate to pan.
  3. Once chocolate has melted into a creamy consistency, add milk.
  4. Use a whisk to mix milk and chocolate together
  5. Once the mixture is warm enough to your liking, pour into your favorite mug.
  6. Top your mug off with whipped cream.
  7. Sprinkle a bit of cinnamon onto the whipped cream.
  8. Enjoy!
